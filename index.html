About Colin

Welcome! I'm Colin, an author driven by exploring the spaces where real-world human experience intersects with compelling structure. Drawing on nearly 40 years of paramedic experience across New Zealand, Australia, and the UK, my writing navigates intense frontline realities, human resilience, and the critical systems that support personal recovery.

My work crosses distinct spaces—from tension-filled hospital thrillers and illuminating fictional biographies to science-backed stress-management frameworks designed to bring clinical strategies out into everyday life. Across all my projects, I value delivering professional, authoritative depth balanced with a friendly, welcoming, and open narrative voice.
